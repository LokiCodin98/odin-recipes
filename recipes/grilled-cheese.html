Grilled Cheese

Home

Description

A grilled sandwich with the need of a slice of cheese and butter to make the perfect combo that you can never imagine.

This is a fan favorite everywhere and a kid go to for simplicity, this is something you have to learn before you burn the next attempt!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar preferred)
  • 3 tablespoons of butter, divided

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients
  2. Step 2: Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side each bread slice.
  3. Step 3: Place 1 slice bread, butter-side down, in the hot skillet; top with 1 slice cheese.
  4. Step 4: Place 1 slice bread, butter-side up, on top cheese.
  5. Step 5: Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
  6. Step 6: Repeat with remaining bread and cheese slices. Serve and enjoy!
