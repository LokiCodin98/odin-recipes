Description

A perfect meal in the morning that is filling and also sweet at the same time.

Easy to make, and the matter is paying attention to the dish and waiting for certain times to flip.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg, beaten

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather the ingredients
  2. Step 2: Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl; make a well in the center. Pour in milk, oil, and egg; mix until smooth.
  3. Step 3: Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop about ¼ cup batter per pancake onto the griddle; cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, 1 to 2 minutes. Flip; cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
  4. Step 4: Serve hot and enjoy!
